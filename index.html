What is Evapotranspiration? 什么是蒸散发？

Evapotranspiration (ET) is the process by which water is transferred from the land to the atmosphere by evaporation from the soil and other surfaces and by transpiration from plants. 蒸散发（ET） 是指水分从陆地表面蒸发到大气中的过程

🌱

Plant Transpiration 植物蒸腾

Plants absorb water through roots, transport it through stems, and release it as vapor through leaf stomata 植物通过根系吸收水分，经过茎秆传输，最终通过叶片气孔释放水蒸气

💧

Soil Evaporation 土壤蒸发

Direct evaporation of soil moisture into the air, influenced by temperature, humidity, and wind speed 土壤表面的水分直接蒸发到空气中

🌡️ Cooling Principle: Water phase change (liquid → gas) requires heat absorption (latent heat of vaporization ~2450 kJ/kg). When plants transpire, this heat is absorbed from the surrounding environment, lowering air temperature. 🌡️ 降温原理： 水分从液态变为气态需要吸收热量（汽化潜热，约2450 kJ/kg）。 当植物蒸腾时，这部分热量从周围环境中吸收，从而降低空气温度。

🏙️

Urban Heat Island 城市热岛效应

Urban areas are 1-5°C warmer than surrounding rural areas, potentially reaching 10°C under extreme conditions 城市地区温度比周围郊区高 1-5°C，极端情况下甚至高出 10°C

  • Increased AC demand 增加空调用电需求
  • Worse air quality 加剧空气污染
  • Health risks 威胁居民健康
🌳

Nature's Solution 植被的解决方案

Vegetation cools cities through shade and evapotranspiration—a natural and sustainable approach 植被通过遮荫和蒸散发降低城市温度——自然且可持续的方式

  • Lower energy use 降低建筑制冷能耗
  • Better air quality 改善空气质量
  • Improved wellbeing 提升居民福祉
📊

  • ROI evaluation 评估投资回报率
  • Policy guidance 制定政策建议
